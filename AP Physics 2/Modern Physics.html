General Relativity:

  • Acceleration is accompanied by forces, the observer that is accelerating feels the time dilation and the length contraction.
  • While Special Relativity deals with differences in velocities, it's all inertial. General Relativity deals with acceleration.
  • Accelerating feels like an external gravitational field, and all physics falls under this equivalence principle.
  • If you accelerate at a relativistic rate, a light beam will curve as if it were undergoing projectile motion. Therefore we can deduce that gravtiy makes light curve (this effect is known as Gravitational Lensing)
